About Acacia Lounge

Welcome to Acacia Lounge, where sophistication meets relaxation in the heart of Kenya. Established with a vision to create an exceptional social experience, we pride ourselves on offering an extensive selection of premium beverages in an elegant and inviting atmosphere.

Our expert mixologists craft each drink with precision and creativity, using only the finest ingredients to ensure every sip is memorable. From classic cocktails to innovative creations, our menu is designed to delight even the most discerning palates.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Acacia Lounge began as a small neighborhood bar with a simple mission: to provide a welcoming space where friends could gather, relax, and enjoy quality drinks. Over the years, we've grown into one of Nairobi's premier destinations for cocktail enthusiasts, but we've never lost sight of our roots.

Our Philosophy

We believe that great drinks are more than just beverages - they're experiences. That's why we source the finest ingredients, from locally harvested fruits to internationally acclaimed spirits. Our team continuously trains to stay at the forefront of mixology trends while honoring classic techniques.

The Acacia Experience

At Acacia Lounge, every visit is special. Our warm and knowledgeable staff are dedicated to providing exceptional service, making your time with us an unforgettable experience. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, conducting business, or simply unwinding after a long day, we've created the perfect ambiance for every moment.
